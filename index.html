About Our School

Hansvahini Convent School, Alampur, Dist-Rohtas, PS-Sheosagar, Sasaram is an English Medium Co-Educational School of high standard has come into existence in January 2010. It is run and managed by HANSVAHINI Education and Social Welfare Foundation holds ISO 9001:2015 certification. This school has been established with the aim of service to the nation by providing for the best education in home like environment.

Our Mission

To provide quality education in a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment that inspires curiosity, creativity, and confidence. We aim to develop responsible, compassionate, and independent individuals equipped with the skills and values to thrive in a global society.

Our Vision

To shape young minds into confident, independent individuals through real-life education in a nurturing, value-driven environment. We envision a future where every child realizes their true potential and contributes meaningfully to society.

Our Values

At Hansvahini Convent School, we stand for: Integrity, Perseverance, Civic Responsibility, Cultural Respect & Holistic Growth.

Our Academic Programs

If our school is a pen, the parents are the ink, joining together we can write the success story of educational development of our children.

Pre-Primary School

The approach at Pre Primary is 'Child Centric'. "Play While Learn and Learn While Play" is the main objective at this level. Emphasis is given on imparting knowledge of the world around the child, developing linguistic & creative skills, fine & gross motor skills.

Primary School

The approach at this level is 'Learning by Doing'. A thematic approach is followed to stimulate children through interactive sessions, projects, surveys and other innovative activities. Emphasis on participation of each student in the curricular & co-curricular activities is given along with the strong sense of individuality.

Junior School

It follows an enriched curriculum with in-depth knowledge of the subjects. Besides following the NCERT guidelines, the school keeps an open mind to the changing educational patterns. It stresses upon developing right study habits, value education, overall personality development and coping with peer pressure at this stage.

STEM Program

Advanced courses in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics.

Director's Message

Mr. Nitesh Singh

Director

"If our school is a pen, the parents are the ink; joining together we can write the success story of educational development of our children."

Our teachers are well qualified & share a passion for teaching, thereby bringing out the best in both themselves and their students. We edify our students in classical as well as contemporary models of learning while giving them a solid foundation in cultural insights of the world.

We pride ourselves on creating a culture of learning where students are free to express or share their interest in variety of fields while developing their character as well. Delayed gratification, integrity, civic consciousness and the willingness not to give up, these are the values that we seek to inculcate in our students. With extra-curricular classes, a strong emphasis on communication skills and activities mean to foster cultural understanding, the students of our school emerges as elucidated and responsible citizen of the world.

Hansvahini Convent School is more than an ordinary school. We encourage students to embark on difficult, challenging journeys that stretch their potential. But we do so with an unyielding faith in capacities of our teachers and students. The management and staff of Success Kids Academy is deeply committed to work tirelessly in order to make our students’ learning experience an exciting and elucidating one.
